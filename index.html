About Us

Our Hospital Story

Because We Care

ChanRe Rheumatology and Immunology Center & Research is a unique one of its kind hospital in India dedicated for management of patients suffering from musculoskeletal disease (rheumatology) and other immunological diseases

CRICR was established on 12th Dec 2002 by Dr.Chandrashekara.S Rheumatologist. It started as a small out-patient clinic, and has now developed into a complete facility with a compressive laboratory. In 8th year we have successfully started an in-patient Facility at Basaveshwarnagar.

Read more

our service

It provides under one roof a complete care to the patients suffering from Arthritis and other Immunological Diseases such as immune deficiency disorders, allergic disorders and immune-hematological disorder,

Rheumatology & Immunology

The Rheumatology and immunology department is one of its kind specialty clinic spearheaded by experienced immunologist and rheumatologists .

Immuno Deficiency

The department is conceptualized to provide high quality and compassionate care for patients with Immunodeficiency syndromes.

Joint Preservation and Restoration

This is an integrated consultation system,providing orthopedic expertise for preservation and restoration of joints.

Integrated Pain Management (IPM) Center

Pain is the most common and challenging symptom for all musculoskeletal disorders.

Allergy

Allergic respiratory disorders in particular asthma and respiratory tract infections are increasing in the developed and in developing countries.

Physiotherapy

Our Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation center provides a full range of services to help patients prevent injury and disease, recover function and strength, and reduce pain
